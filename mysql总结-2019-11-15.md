```
# my.cnf
server-id=2
relay-log=/a/b/c/relay.log

> change master to

master_password="123456",
master_port=3306,
master_log_file='mysql_binlog.000004',
master_log_pos=593,
master_connect_retry=10;

reset slave|start slave|stop slave


```
